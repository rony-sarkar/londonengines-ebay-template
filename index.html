Product Description

Exchange Basis

All engines are sold on an exchange basis. This means that you must trade in your old engine for the new one. The price quoted is for the head, block, and sump only. Additional charges may apply for other parts.

Ancillaries

All ancillaries are removed from the vehicle's engine and placed onto the supplied engine. This includes the alternator, starter, water pump, power steering pump, and air conditioning compressor. If any of these parts are found to be faulty during the fitting process, you will be notified immediately and we will work to resolve the issue as quickly as possible.

Collection

We can arrange for the collection of your vehicle from any location. We do not have our own recovery truck, but we work with a number of companies that can collect your vehicle quickly, even the same day. Our turnaround time is typically between 5 and 7 working days.

Vehicle Service

After you have collected your vehicle, you will be required to bring it back to us for a 1000-mile service. This is so that we can inspect the engine, make sure it is working properly, and change the service parts.

Warranty

We provide a warranty on all engines that we supply and fit. The length of the warranty will be agreed upon at the time of purchase. Please note that the warranty is not transferable and we are not liable for any vehicle hire or additional charges.
